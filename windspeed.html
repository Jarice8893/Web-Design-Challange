WIND SPEED


This plot represents Wind Speed Vs Latitude, we can see that in between -60 to 80 latitude is when it takes effect the most. The higher the wind speed the higher the latitude, which means North Hempisphere experiences higher winds. Wind Speed helps carry mositure in our atmosphere which can monitor and predict weather patterns such as storm surges.
