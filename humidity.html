Humidity


In this plot, we have Humidity vs Latitude. As you can see the higher the humidity percentage and the closer to the Equator is where the humidity is at it's peak, almost a straight line of 100% in the middle of plot. Humidity is a type of atmospheric moisture caused by water vapor evaporating when heat is applied. Which means the higher the heat the longer warm moisture stays on our skin and the hotter we get.
